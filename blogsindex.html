Lemon Garlic Grilled Shrimp Recipe

Indulge in a flavorful seafood delight!

Benefits of Lemon Garlic Grilled Shrimp

  • High in protein and low in calories, making it a healthy option for any meal.
  • The combination of lemon and garlic adds flavor without the need for heavy sauces or marinades.
  • Rich in antioxidants and essential nutrients, supporting overall health and well-being.
  • Grilling enhances the natural sweetness of the shrimp while imparting a smoky charred flavor.
  • Quick and easy to prepare, perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings with friends.
  • A versatile dish that can be served as an appetizer, main course, or added to salads and pasta dishes.

Why You'll Love This Recipe

  • Quick and easy to make
  • Bursting with vibrant flavors
  • Perfect for weeknight dinners or entertaining
  • Healthy and nutritious

Tips for Perfect Lemon Garlic Grilled Shrimp

  • Use fresh, high-quality ingredients for the best flavor.
  • Don't overcook the shrimp - they should be pink and opaque.
  • Marinate the shrimp for at least 15 minutes for maximum flavor.
  • Preheat the grill to medium-high heat before cooking.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor.

Let's Get Cooking!

Follow these simple steps to make delicious Lemon Garlic Grilled Shrimp:

  1. In a bowl, mix together garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat evenly. Marinate for 15-30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Thread shrimp onto skewers.
  4. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and opaque.
  5. Remove from grill and garnish with fresh parsley, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
